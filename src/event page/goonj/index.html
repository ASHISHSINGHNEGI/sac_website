About

Goonj is the flagship cultural event of GB Pant Institute. It brings together students, faculty, and staff to celebrate the vibrant and diverse cultural heritage of our college. The event showcases various art forms, performances, competitions, and exhibitions. Through Goonj, we aim to provide a platform for students to express their talents, celebrate creativity, and foster a sense of community. Join us for an unforgettable experience filled with music, dance, drama, and much more!
